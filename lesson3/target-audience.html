Target Audience

The target audience of this website may be:

People who want to have an active participation in the commerce of Santa Fe, and by this way achieve a high increase in their sales volume, and also get noticed by the community. It is very likely that most of the site's audience is made up of people who is starting a business and need some boost to grow.

Personas:

  • Persona 1:

    A salesman who wants his or her business to get noticed in the city.

  • Persona 2:

    A person who is planning to move to the city and thinks about job opportunities.

Scenarios

  • What commerces related to my business are in the city?

  • What events people are going to be focused on?

  • I want to get a job at a certain field

  • How did my political management contribute to the growth of the economy and development?

  • I need to find a company willing to support my starting business
